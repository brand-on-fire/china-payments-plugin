Frequently asked questions

What's the total cost of using the plugin?

The free version has a small 2% transaction fee on top of Stripe's standard fees to help us continue supporting the WordPress community. The paid version is $99/year and charges no transaction fees on top of Stripe's standard fees.

Do I need a Stripe.com account to use this plugin?

Yes. Please sign up for a Stripe.com account in your country.

What payment options are currently supported?

WeChat Pay and Alipay payments are supported in the free version with a small 2% transaction fee on top of Stripe fees. You can upgrade to the PRO version to remove the transaction fee.

Does the plugin support WooCommerce?

Yes! WeChat Pay and Alipay payments are fully supported with WooCommerce.

Is the license transferable?

Yes! Your license is fully transferable across sites. It simply needs to be deactivated in the old website's WordPress Dashboard or in your Account area.
